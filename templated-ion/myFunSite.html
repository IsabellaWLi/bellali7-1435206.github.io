How it works

It's simple

Packages

With a push of a button, you can send premade packages to whoever you'd like. It's a flat rate fee for each box size. There are various options of premade packages, from snacks to school supplies. These will be delivered within 3 days. Any custom packages will take up to 7 days.

Messages

Our app allows people to have conversations with each other. Leaving memos is a great way to connect on a deeper level. Special sticker can be used to display your feelings and emotions. These messages can be saved onto your account with a press of a button.

Photos

If messaging and packages are not enough, photos can be sent to each other through our app. These can be kept and saved into your phone. These photos can be specially made so it looks like you are with your partner or friend. It allows for the other to feel like they are with you.

Our goal

Building old relationships

Our program allows you to build old relationships, even when you're far apart. We strive to provide a innovative platform for you to connect with old friends. Our unique features allow you to build your relationship on a deeper level.
